Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

Do I need to be in shape to start?

+

Not at all. Orangetheory is designed for all fitness levels. Our coaches modify every exercise so whether you're a beginner or an athlete, you'll get a workout tailored to YOU.

What should I bring to my first class?

+

Just yourself, workout clothes, and water. We provide everything else including the heart rate monitor for your first session. Arrive 15 minutes early so we can get you set up.

How long is each class?

+

Each class is approximately 60 minutes and includes a mix of treadmill, rowing, and floor exercises. Every class is different so you'll never get bored.

What are VIP sessions?

+

VIP sessions are a special introductory package of 5 classes at a discounted rate. It's the best way to experience Orangetheory and see results before committing to a membership.

Can I try a different studio each time?

+

Absolutely! Your VIP pass works across all 7 of our Arizona studios. Try different locations and coaches to find your perfect fit.
